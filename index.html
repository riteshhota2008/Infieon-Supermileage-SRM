About us

Who we are

Infieon Supermileage is the official team of SRM University participating in the Shell Eco-Marathon.

What we do

The team comprises of students from different engineering domains who have come together to build highly fuel efficient vehicles. The team is gearing up for the 2016 challenge and will be participating in the Prototype - Gasoline category.

What we believe in

The team also plans to spread the message of 'Fuel and Energy Conservation' through its various campaigns around the country. The team believes in continuously learning and of course, just as its motto goes for the 2015-16 season....

"Journey is the Destination!"
