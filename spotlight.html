July 2014: Renowned wildlife conservationist Ms. Prerna Bindra lends support to Infieon Supermileage

Ms. Prerna Bindra is one of the most renowned wildlife conservationists in India. Click here to see her website. Former Member, National Board for Wildlife and its core Standing Committee (2010-13) Member, State Board for Wildlife, Uttarakhand. Conservation Partner, Wildlife Conservation Society-India. Editor, TigerLink. Trustee & Founder Director, Bagh

She has worked with various publications including India Today, The Asian Age, The Week, The Pioneer, Sanctuary Asia, Tehelka and The Times of India— penning well over a thousand articles on nature and wildlife.

She has authored ‘The King and I: Travels in Tigerland‘ ,was given the ABN-AMRO-Sanctuary Asia Wildlife Service Award for in-depth and consistent coverage of conservation issues, and the Carl Zeiss Award for Wildlife Conservation for excellence in networking and engaging the public at large for the cause of wildlife.
