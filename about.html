About SRM University

Started in 1985 , and now ranked among the top universities in India, SRM University provides a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes in the fields of Engineering, Management, Medicine and Health Sciences and Science and Humanities.

The large 600 acres campus includes state of the art facilities including world class libraries, Wi- Fi Campus, a 5000 capacity AC auditorium, online smart classrooms, Hostels with premium facilities, and endless convenience on campus including bookstores, gym, prayer halls, ATMS etc.

The collaboration with worldreknowned universities helps to improve the curriculum and set global standards. The collaboration works in tandem with programmes like the Dual Degree programme, Twining (Transfer) Degree overseas , Faculty Abroad Programme and the reputed Semester Abroad Programme (SAP) which allows students of the university to attend courses in the top universities of the world like MIT, Carnegie Mellon, UC Davis, Warwick to name a few. The university has accreditons from the national agencies like the UGC , AICTE and boasts a ‘A’ grade from the NAAC. Some of the courses additionally have an ABET accrediton while others have applied for the same.

The University encourages the students to employ a research oriented methodology and thus has numerous teams working on reputed projects like the SRM SAT.

The University justifies its moto to” Learn Leap and Lead” as it provides an exemplary support in the from of well versed faculty , state of the art labs, global exposure and a research conducive environment.

Vision and Mission

Vision: To emerge as a World - Class University in creating and disseminating knowledge, and providing students a unique learning experience in science, technology, medicine, management and other areas of scholarship that will best serve the world and betterment of mankind.

Mission: Move up through international alliances and collaborative initiatives to achieve global excellence. Accomplish a process to advance knowledge in a rigorous academic and research environment.

About the Department

Started in 2004, the objective of the B.Tech. Program in Automobile Engineering is to broaden the exposure in the design, development and testing of automobiles. Furthermore, the program trains students to meet the technological challenges and diverse needs of the industry and society in various areas of automobile engineering and equips them to excel in a truly competitive industry.

Over the years, the department has grown exponentially and now it is one of the best universities offering Automobile Engineering.
