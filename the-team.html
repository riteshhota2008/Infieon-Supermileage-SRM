About Us

Infieon Supermileage is a team based in SRM University, Chennai. We aim to participate in Shell Eco Marathon - Asia 2015. The team was formed way back in 2012 with the aim of participating in Shell Eco Marathon-Asia by building a highly fuel efficient Vehicle. Due to unforeseen circumstances, the 2013 event was cancelled by Shell citing safety concerns. Undeterred, the team kept its morale high and successfully worked in 2013 towards participating in the 2014 event. The team also held its head high by successfully participating in the 2014 event.

Now, members of Infieon Supermileage are working hard for participating in the 2015 event to be held in Manila. The team will be participating in the Gasoline Prototype category at the Shell Eco Marathon - which is among the most competitive categories. The team also plans to spread the message of Energy and Fuel Efficiency through its various campaigns around the country.

The team this year comprises of the Director (E&T) as the nominal head, followed by the HOD of Department of Automobile Engineering as the Faculty Adviser, the Team Captain and other Technical and Corporate members. The Technical team is further divided into 2 sub-domains- Mechanical and Electrical.

Buoyed by skilled technical expertise comprising of efficient future engineers with a research oriented mindset and a swashbuckling corporate team with some of the finest leadership potential you'd come across in the Asian subcontinent, the team is all set to take Shell Eco Marathon by storm in the years to come and leave an indelible mark.

The team believes in continuously learning and of course, just as its motto goes for the 2014-15 season....

"Journey is the destination!"
