Project 2015



This March, the team proudly announced the commencement of its next project - Project 2015. The team plans to beat its own performance both in terms of mileage and reach. The team has started working for the next car and is also working on a strategized campaign to educate the masses about 'Fuel and Energy Efficiency' . The motto for Project 2015 is 'Journey is the Destination', through which the team emphasizes on the learning outcomes of next year's journey to the competition.
